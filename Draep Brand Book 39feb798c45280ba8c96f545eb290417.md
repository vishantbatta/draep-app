## 1. The Logo

### The symbol

The Draep mark is a **measuring tape looped into the Greek letter alpha (α)** — the tailor's most trusted tool, drawn as the symbol of a first. Alpha stands for the beginning, the leader, the highest standard: Draep as the alpha of custom tailoring.

- **The alpha loop**: the tape crosses over itself to form α — precision folded into ambition, the first standard in an industry that never had one.
- **The ticks**: measurement markings along the tape edge — the brand's obsession with accuracy, visible at every size.
- **The rivet**: the metal tape-end with its punched hole — a nod to real tailor's tools, grounding the mark in craft.
- **The gradient**: light wraps around the tape from bright saffron to deep ember, giving the flat tool dimension and energy.

### The wordmark

“draep” is set in lowercase in a **rounded geometric sans** — approachable, modern, and confident. The lowercase treatment keeps the brand humble and friendly; the heavy weight keeps it dependable.

### Lockups

- **Primary**: symbol + wordmark, horizontal (as above). Default for all uses.
- **Symbol only**: app icon, favicons, social avatars, watermarks.
- **Wordmark only**: when the symbol appears separately nearby (e.g., packaging tape).
- **Reversed**: white wordmark + full-color symbol on Ink Navy backgrounds.

### Clearspace & minimum size

- **Clearspace**: keep a margin equal to the height of the wordmark's “d” bowl on all sides.
- **Minimum size**: symbol never below 24px tall on screen (8mm in print); primary lockup never below 96px wide.

### Logo don'ts

- Don't recolor the tape or flatten its gradient to a single orange.
- Don't set the wordmark in another typeface or in uppercase.
- Don't rotate, outline, add shadows, or stretch the lockup.
- Don't place the full-color logo on orange or busy photographic backgrounds — use the reversed version on navy or a white holding shape.
- Don't separate the ticks from the tape — the ticks only exist on the tape.

## 2. Brand Foundation

### Mission

Simplify custom tailoring through technology — seamless design selection, accurate digital measurements, transparent pricing, and consistent quality across every order.

### Vision

Redefine the global tailoring industry by building the most trusted technology ecosystem connecting customers, tailors, and fashion — through precision, innovation, and craftsmanship.

### Brand idea

**“Measured for you.”** — Everything Draep does starts with a measurement: of your body, of your style, of a promise kept. The measuring tape in the logo is the brand's entire philosophy in one object.

### Core values

| Value | How it shows up |
| --- | --- |
| Precision | Every measurement matters — taken by SOP, stored forever in the vault |
| Trust | Transparent pricing, written SLAs, and the Draep Protection Policy |
| Craftsmanship | Skilled tailors celebrated and paid fairly, never hidden |
| Innovation | Technology that simplifies a fragmented industry |
| Customer First | Bespoke made easy, accessible, and enjoyable |
| Consistency | The same high standard in every home, every city |

### Personality

**Warm precision.** Draep is the expert who makes you feel at ease — as exact as an engineer, as welcoming as a good host. Never cold, never sloppy.

## 3. Voice & Tone

- **Confident, not boastful**: state promises plainly — “Delivered by March 3rd” beats “super-fast delivery!”
- **Warm, not cutesy**: speak like a trusted Style Captain, not a mascot.
- **Precise, not jargon-heavy**: use real numbers (dates, sizes, prices) instead of vague claims.
- **Respectful of craft**: tailors are “master tailors” and partners — never “vendors” or “labor.”

| Say | Not |
| --- | --- |
| Your blouse will be ready for trial on Saturday, 6–9 PM. | Your order will be delivered soon! |
| Measured once, remembered forever. | AI-powered measurement cloud solution. |
| Your saree is protected — in writing. | Don't worry, we've got you covered 😉 |

## 4. Color System

All colors are sampled directly from the logo.

### Primary palette

| Color | Hex | Usage |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Ink Navy | #083068 | Wordmark, headlines, body text, primary dark surfaces |
| Draep Orange | #F89010 | Primary actions, brand accents, the tape |
| Ember | #D06010 | Hover/pressed states, gradient depth |
| Tape Silver | #D8D8D8 | Dividers, secondary strokes (the tape's underside) |
| Chalk White | #FFFFFF | Backgrounds, reversed text |

### The Tape Gradient

The signature brand gradient follows the light wrapping around the tape:

**#F89010 → #E87810 → #D06010 → #A85010** (135°, top-left to bottom-right). Use it on hero surfaces, primary buttons, and the symbol itself — never on body text.

### Extended tints

- **Navy tints**: #083068 → #3A5C8F (interactive) → #9FB3CF (disabled) → #EAF0F8 (navy-tinted backgrounds)
- **Orange tints**: #F89010 → #FBB95E (highlights) → #FDE3BF (subtle fills) → #FFF6EA (warm backgrounds)

### Semantic colors

| State | Hex | Usage |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Success | #16A34A | Trial passed, order closed, payment confirmed |
| Warning | #EAB308 | Approaching SLA, action needed |
| Error | #DC2626 | QC failure, missed slot, disputes |
| Info | #2563EB | Status updates, tips |

### Usage ratio & accessibility

- **60 / 30 / 10**: white and warm neutrals 60%, Ink Navy 30%, Draep Orange 10%. Orange is the spice, not the base.
- Body text is always **Ink Navy on white** (13.9:1 contrast).
- Never set small text in orange on white; on orange surfaces, use white text at 16px+ bold or Ink Navy text.
- Orange + navy together is the brand handshake — every key screen should contain both.

## 5. Typography

The wordmark's rounded geometric letterforms set the typographic direction.

| Role | Typeface | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Headings | Poppins (SemiBold / Bold) | Rounded geometry echoing the wordmark; sentence case only |
| Body | Inter (Regular / Medium) | High legibility for dense order and catalogue UI |
| Measurements & data | IBM Plex Mono | Sizes, order IDs, prices, SLAs — tabular numerals always |

### Type scale (mobile-first)

- Display 32/38 · H1 24/30 · H2 20/26 · H3 17/24 · Body 15/22 · Caption 13/18 · Data 14/20 mono
- Headings in Ink Navy; one orange highlight word per headline maximum.

## 6. Iconography & Graphic Motifs

- **Tick marks**: the tape's measurement ticks are the core graphic motif — use as section dividers, progress indicators, and list bullets.
- **The curl**: circular crops and curled-ribbon shapes derived from the symbol frame photography and stats.
- **The rivet dot**: a small filled circle terminates lines and timelines — every journey ends with the tape-end.
- **Icon style**: 2px rounded strokes, navy by default, orange for the active state; corners rounded to match the tape's curve.
- **Photography**: warm natural light, real homes and real hands — tape, chalk, fabric textures; never sterile studio mannequins.

## 7. Motion

- **Tape Unroll**: 600ms ease-out — panels and progress bars extend like a tape being drawn.
- **Tick**: 200ms steps — counters and measurements animate in tick increments, not smooth glides.
- **Curl In**: 500ms ease-in-out — cards enter with a subtle arc, echoing the symbol's loop.
- **Rivet Pulse**: 2s gentle pulse on the active step of the order timeline.
- Durations: 200ms micro · 400–600ms transitions · nothing over 800ms.

## 8. Component Guidelines

- **Border radius**: 12px cards · pill buttons (the tape has no sharp corners).
- **Primary button**: Tape Gradient fill, white bold label; pressed state shifts to Ember.
- **Secondary button**: 1.5px Ink Navy outline, navy label, transparent fill.
- **Order timeline**: a horizontal measuring tape — ticks for steps, rivet dot for the current step.
- **Dividers**: Tape Silver 1px lines with optional tick pattern.
- **Shadows**: soft warm shadow (navy at 8% opacity), single elevation level.
- **Forms**: measurement fields always paired with mono numerals and unit labels (in / cm).

## 9. Applications

- **App icon**: symbol only, white curl on Tape Gradient tile.
- **WhatsApp comms**: navy text, one orange CTA line, order updates formatted with tick bullets.
- **Collection kit**: navy waterproof kit with orange tape-stripe running around it like a wrapped measuring tape; wordmark in white.
- **Style Captain ID & uniform**: navy base, orange lanyard, symbol-only badge — the tape identifies them at the door.
- **Invoices & rate cards**: white base, navy tables, orange totals row; mono numerals throughout.
- **Social**: orange stat highlights on navy tiles; the curl frames customer stories.
